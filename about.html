In the summer 2018, I had the opportunity to work with Prof. Stefan Leutenegger at the Dyson Robotics Lab at Imperial College London, on visual servoing for aerial additive building manufacturing (ABM). In summer 2017, I worked with Prof. Sebastian Scherer at the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, in an aerial inspection setup where we developed a vision-inspired framework for the robust localization of radioactive sources, from an aerial vehicle, for the multimodal mapping of a nuclear facility.

I was a part of the IITB Mars Rover Team, which aspires to build rover prototypes capable of extraterrestrial exploration.
